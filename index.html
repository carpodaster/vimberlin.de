Vimberlin - Vim User Group Berlin

Vimberlin is gathering for Vim-enthusiasts in Berlin. Every odd month we are giving a meeting for advanced users and every even month there is a workshop for absolute beginners to Vim - welcome! (Thanks wikimatze for the picture).
